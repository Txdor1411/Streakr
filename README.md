<summary>Motivation for this project</summary>

### Why build "another habit tracking app"?

As I said in the first paragraph, my coleagues and I get along really well and we usually create a healthy sense of competition around objectives / goals. We first started to track our habits in a shared google sheet and send photos on social media apps in order to prove that we completed the habit. It was a tedious process and it was pretty obvious that we were going to get bored and stop tracking.

That's when the idea struck me: why not build a social media + habit tracking app which helps people hold each other accountable? It didn't have to be big, just for my friend group at first!
And I got to work...

</details>

<summary>How does this work?</summary>

### TLDR; You create habits, add friends and post videos/photos to prove it

It has never been easier to share your achievements with friends and keep each other accountable! After creating an account and mutually following someone, you can see their activity and goals. There are also some nice finishing toucher, where you can react to others' posts or "Nudge" them if they've been skipping it lately.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Why not use Instagram or other apps?</summary>

### TLDR; Instagram is very distracting and you might not like random people seeing your daily life.

Streakr offers better privacy and a sense of mutual aid within it's community.
After all, it's much easier to complete & track habits after seeing a feed full of committed people rather than scrolling endless social media feeds.

</details>
